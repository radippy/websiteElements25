What is Website Aesthetics?

Aesthetics

A set of principles concerned with the nature and appreciation of beauty. Beauty is one of the main subjects of aesthetics, together with art and taste. Many of its definitions include the idea that an object is beautiful if perceiving it is accompanied by aesthetic pleasure. Among the examples of beautiful objects are landscapes, sunsets, humans and works of art. To be aesthetic is to be pleasurable to look at or perceived as beautiful by the viewer.
